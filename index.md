Hi, my name is Greg and I'm an audio engineer living in Chicago. I primarily work as a mastering engineer at Chicago Mastering Service where I've mastered albums for artists on Sacred Bones, Numero Group, Fire Talk, RVNG Intl., Exploding in Sound, and International Anthem to name a few. I also record, mix, and do live sound both locally and on the road. I've been in bands like Yeesh, Clearance, and currently Stuck. Lastly, when I'm not making the hits, I'm movin' units with Born Yesterday Records. Here is a partial discography of records I've had the pleasure of working on.
