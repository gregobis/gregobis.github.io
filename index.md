Hi, my name is Greg and I'm an audio engineer living in Chicago. I am a mastering engineer at Chicago Mastering Service where I've mastered albums for artists on Sacred Bones, Numero Group, Fire Talk, RVNG Intl., Exploding in Sound, and International Anthem to name a few. I also record, mix, and do live sound both locally and abroad. I've been in bands like Yeesh, Clearance, and currently Stuck. I am also movin' units as co-owner of Born Yesterday Records.
