Hi, my name is Greg and I'm an audio engineer in Chicago, IL. I work at Chicago Mastering Service where I've mastered on albums for artists on labels like Sub Pop, Matador, ANTI-, Car Park, Sacred Bones, Numero Group, Fire Talk, and International Anthem to name a few. I record and mix records too. I also do front of house on tour. I play in the band Stuck, and formerly played in Yeesh and Clearance. I am also "moving units" as co-owner of Born Yesterday Records. Lastly, I am a budding sound designer. Here is a very limited discography of records I've had the pleasure of working on. I would love to work on yours too!
